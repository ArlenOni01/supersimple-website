PangeaEatery

Before the end of grade 5 in elementary school, I would wake up early to prepare my breakfast and pack my lunch, thus saving my parents the time to do those things for me. I did this all through Middle School to the end of y High School years. This culminated in my real love of cooking which began when I would stay up until very late at night just watching simple cooking videos and getting very intrigued with it as it was, to me, an art style. So when I came up with the idea of "Pangea", I felt as though the best way to reach out to people would be through my food. Because after all, who doesn't love FOOD! My goal is to simply bring flavors from all around the globe together into one restaurant similarly to the way pangea was once all of the world in one land mass.
