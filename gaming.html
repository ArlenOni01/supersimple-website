Game Development

From my favorite video games, to video games I have made, I have a passion for gaming that I never really realized until 2020.

I began developing my own game in the summer of 2022 as a side project of mine. I wanted to be able to have something of some merit that was mine that was worth something related to the field that I want to go in to. Using the Unity engine, I coded a game in C# and used 2D tile-maps to construct a dungeon-esque game where the objective of the game is to go around the map, solving riddles and defeating enemies. Once you have solved all of the riddles, then you have beaten the game.
